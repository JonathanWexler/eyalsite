The Firm

Sharon Law, P.A. is a boutique firm dedicated to advise and advance its clients’ corporate governance, business management, and business transactions. At Sharon Law, P.A., we pride ourselves on efficient, effective, and reliable legal consulting services. We understand that the keys to a successful business are proper business formation and proper management practices.

We understand that like anything else, the legal service industry is not a “one size fits all.” Therefore, at Sharon Law, P.A. we customize our legal services and costs to the needs of our clients and not vice-versa. Yet regardless of the size of the clients’ business, Sharon Law, P.A. seeks to provide high quality legal consulting, personal interaction, and efficient cost.

Our goal is to assist and advise our clients regarding adequate corporate policies, proper business development and management, and transactional consulting. Sharon Law, P.A. also provides its corporate and business clients with ongoing general counsel services, as well as business consulting regarding business operations and transactions.

Sharon Law, P.A. was formed with the understanding that corporate clients are cognizant of their consulting and legal costs. Our mission is to provide corporate and businesses clients with sophisticated consulting and customized work product at a cost effective approach. Therefore, Sharon Law, P.A. is dedicated to provide its clients with professional corporate and business consulting at a reasonable market rates.

Attorney Profile

Mr. Eyal Sharon, Esquire, is the founder and managing partner of Sharon Law, P.A. Mr. Sharon has several years of complex litigation, corporate, and business consulting experience. Mr. Sharon currently represents corporate entities and start-ups in domestic and international business transactions and serves as a business law consultant and outside general counsel for several U.S. companies. Along with his passion for legal consulting and advocacy, Mr. Sharon is dedicated to make an impact in the South Florida community.

Mr. Sharon began his legal career as a law clerk at the Florida Attorney General’s Antitrust Division. Mr. Sharon left the Attorney General Office to pursue private practice as an associate attorney at Greenspoon Marder, P.A.’s Complex Commercial Litigation Group. As part of his practice, Mr. Sharon represented corporate clients in state, federal, and ADR proceedings. In addition, Mr. Sharon advised his corporate clients on corporate practices and governance, as well as in various business transactions. Mr. Sharon assisted his corporate clients with contract review and revisions, consumer and business based transactions, and ongoing business transactions.

Mr. Sharon holds a B.A. degree, summa cum laude, from Temple University, where he graduated top of his class and as president scholar. At Temple University Mr. Sharon dedicated his academic career on the interaction between governing principles and economic principles. Along with a major in political science, Mr. Sharon completed Temple University’s highly prestigious certificate program specializing in political economy.

Mr. Sharon holds a J.D. from Florida State University College of Law. At Florida State University, Mr. Sharon focused his legal studies on corporate governance and business transactions. In addition to his legal studies, Mr. Sharon served as the Articles Selection Editor and later as a Senior Articles Editor to the highly prestigious Journal of Land Use and Environmental Law. Mr. Sharon is the author of a “Recent Developments” piece that appeared in the Journal of Land Use and Environmental Law. The piece informed members of the legal community on recent decisions and changes to legislation in the world of Land Use and Environmental Law. Mr. Sharon is a recipient of the distinguished pro-bono award for his service to the community during his law school career.

Mr. Sharon is fluent in Hebrew and English. Mr. Sharon is licensed in Florida and New York states. In addition, Mr. Sharon is admitted to appear before the United States District Court for the Southern, Middle, and Northern Districts of Florida.
