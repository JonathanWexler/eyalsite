Attorney Profile

Mr. Eyal Sharon, Esquire, is the founder and managing partner of Sharon Law, P.A. Mr. Sharon is licensed in Florida and New York states, and is admitted to appear before the United States District Court for the Southern, Middle, and Northern Districts of Florida. Mr. Sharon has over three years of complex litigation, corporate, and business consulting experience.

Mr. Sharon began his legal career as a law clerk at the Florida Attorney General’s Antitrust Division. For the past three years, Mr. Sharon served as an associate attorney at Greenspoon Marder, P.A.’s Complex Litigation Group. As part of his practice, Mr. Sharon represented corporate clients in state, federal, and ADR proceedings. In addition, Mr. Sharon advised and assisted his corporate clients on corporate practices and governance, as well as in various business transactions.

Mr. Sharon holds a B.A. degree, summa cum laude, from Temple University, where he graduated top of his class and as president scholar. At Temple University Mr. Sharon dedicated his academic career on the interaction between governing principles and economic principles. Along with a major in political science, Mr. Sharon joined the highly prestigious certificate program specializing in political economy.

Mr. Sharon holds a J.D. from Florida State University College of Law. At Florida State University, Mr. Sharon focused his legal studies on corporate governance and business transactions. In addition to his legal academic studies, Mr. Sharon served as the Articles Selection Editor and later served as a Senior Articles Editor at the highly prestigious Journal of Land Use and Environmental Law. Mr. Sharon is a recipient of the distinguished pro-bono award for his service to the community during his law school career.

Mr. Sharon is fluent in Hebrew and English. He currently represents corporate entities in domestic and international business transactions and serves as a legal consultant for several U.S. companies. Along with his passion for legal consulting and advocacy, Mr. Sharon is dedicated to make an impact in the South Florida community.
