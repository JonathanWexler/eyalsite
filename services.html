Corporate Governance:

Regardless of the size of the business, the nature of business, and the form of the business, effective corporate governance is key. At Sharon Law, P.A. we strive to provide our clients with effective and customized corporate governance advice knowing that effective corporate governance policies are essential to the growth and development of every business.

Our corporate governance representation includes advice and assistance regarding:

Corporate Governance matters, including board constitution, and advice to boards regarding board and committee structure, fiduciary obligations, compliance and oversight, indemnification rights and D&O insurance;

Development of board charters, governance standards, codes of conduct and other corporate programs, policies and procedures;

Composition of and procedures for boards of directors and corporate officers.

Business Consulting:

Every business negotiation and transaction carries with it legal consequences that are often not considered at the time of the transaction or negotiation. At Sharon Law, P.A. we guide and assist our clients through business transactions. The attorneys at Sharon Law, P.A., assist its clients in the development, negotiation, and execution of business transactions.

Our services include advice and assistance regarding:

Corporate, partnership, LLC and other business entity selection, formation, structuring and documentation, to effect optimal governance, ownership, and operational efficiencies;

Guidance on structuring efficient sales, supply, vendor and licensing agreements, consulting and independent contractor arrangements, and other essential business contracts, to protect business interests and serve and effectuate important business relationships;

Executive and employee agreements, plans and benefits including, but not limited to, non-compete and restrictive covenant agreements, stock options, equity and incentive compensation plans;

Formation of joint ventures and strategic arrangements and alliances;

Assistance and advice regarding business regulatory compliance.

Corporate Litigation Support:

At times, corporations and businesses find themselves in the unfortunate situation of litigation. Whether a business gets sued or has a claim against another, litigation can be a stressful process requiring utmost attention from management. Sharon Law, P.A. seeks to ease the process on corporate management by developing and implementing a litigation strategy, as well as providing representation during the litigation. Regardless of whether a business is being sued or has a claim, it is important for an aggressive and effective representation during the litigation.

Our services include:

Representation in Florida and New York state courts;

Representation in Federal Courts;

Representation in ADR proceeding such as mediation and arbitration;

Consulting on trial and appeal strategy.
