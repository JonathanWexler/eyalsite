Business and Commercial Transaction Consulting

Sharon Law, P.A. facilitates our clients’ needs in the development, negotiation, and execution of business transactions. Whether Business-to-Business or Business-to-Consumer, we provide our clients with the legal tools to negotiate and execute transaction in an increasing globalized market.

Our services include advice and assistance regarding:

Corporate, partnership, LLC and other business entity selection, formation, structuring and documentation, to effect optimal governance, ownership, and operational efficiencies;

Guidance on structuring efficient sales, supply, vendor and licensing agreements, consulting and independent contractor arrangements, and other essential business contracts, to protect business interests and serve and effectuate important business relationships;

Executive and employee agreements, plans and benefits including, but not limited to, non-compete and restrictive covenant agreements, stock options, equity and incentive compensation plans;

Formation of joint ventures and strategic arrangements and alliances.

Business Regulation Consulting

Sharon Law, P.A. provides businesses with regulatory consulting. Our goal is to promote our corporate clients’ interest while ensuring regulatory compliance.

Our regulatory consulting includes advice and assistance regarding:

Compliance with consumer regulation and laws.

Compliance with state and federal commercial regulation.

Providing opinion letters to corporate entities on regulatory changes in the industry.

Consumer Transactions Consulting

In a consumer driven economy, the correct approach to consumer transactions may bolster your business and reduce the risk of consumer litigation. As consumer protection laws continue to change, developing policies and procedures in consumer transactions can effectively reduce the risk of consumer litigation and regulatory enforcement actions.

At Sharon Law, P.A., our consumer transaction consulting includes advice and assistance regarding:

Guidance on Consumer-based transactions and negotiations;

Assistance and advice regarding preparation of consumer agreements, terms and conditions, and consumer policies and warranties;

Review and revisions of consumer agreements and policies;

Developing strategies to reduce the risk of consumer based lawsuits.

Corporate Governance Consulting

At Sharon Law, P.A. we provide our clients with effective and customized corporate governance advice. Regardless of the size, nature, and form of the business, effective corporate governance is a key essential to the development and growth of a business.

Our corporate governance consulting includes advice and assistance regarding:

Corporate Governance matters, including board constitution, and advice to boards regarding board and committee structure, fiduciary obligations, compliance and oversight, indemnification rights and D&O insurance;

Development of board charters, governance standards, codes of conduct and other corporate programs, policies and procedures;

Composition of and procedures for boards of directors and corporate officers.

General Counsel Services

In our capacity as outside general counsel we seek to deliver specialized, multi-jurisdictional advice with emphasis on the client’s needs.

Our General Counsel services includes, but not limited to, the following areas:

Labor and Employment Law;

Corporate Governance; See also “Corporate Governance Consulting” section of this website;

Business Law, including business practices, negotiations, and commercial transactions;

Arbitration Law;

Business Regulation;

Consumer Regulation and Practices;

Contract Law;

Business Financing and Venture Capital;

Sale of Goods and Services; and

Consumer and Commercial Contracts.

Real Estate Transaction Consulting

Sharon Law, P.A. provides consulting and facilitation of Real Estate transaction. Our goal is to promote our corporate clients’ interest in the execution of any real estate transaction.

Our Real Estate Transaction consulting includes advice and assistance regarding:

Performing due diligence of prospective real estate deals;

Real Estate Transaction document review, analysis, and advice;

Preparation of real estate transaction agreements, promissory notes, and other closing documents;

Negotiation of Real Estate Transaction terms;

Review of Loan Agreements and loan terms;

“Startups” Practice

Sharon Law, P.A. started with the idea that startups and small businesses will have access to legal counsel services without having to “break the bank” in costs. Sharon Law, P.A. wants to ensure that its services do not come at the expense of its client’s growth. We believe that we have a mutual interest in our client’s growth. Thus, instead of requiring our clients to limit their legal needs based on a firm cost structure, at Sharon Law we customize our legal services and costs to the client.
